Our Story

A Labor of Love

Griya Satya was born from a simple dream: to create spaces where travelers feel truly at home.

Since 2020, we've opened our doors to families, friends, and solo adventurers from all walks of life. What started as two humble guest houses has grown into a beloved haven for those exploring the beauty of Semarang.

Every morning coffee, every shared meal, every story exchanged — these moments remind us why we do what we do. We're not just offering rooms; we're offering a piece of our home and heart.
